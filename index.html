Heath is born, raised, and educated in Virginia. He studied buisness at Bluefield Collage then moved onto Hampden-Sydney Collage where he studied Philosophy and Visual Arts under Dr. Marc Hight and Dr. Pam Fox.

Artist Statement: "In my multimedia practice, I explore the realms of videos and photography, weaving together abstract and performance-based elements. Through the creation of elaborate sets and engaging in roleplay, I delve into the intersection of bright colors with serialist overtones. My work is a contemplation of desire and identity, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in a visual journey that transcends conventional boundaries. "
